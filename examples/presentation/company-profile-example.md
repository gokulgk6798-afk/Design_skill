# Example Presentation: Company Profile Deck

## Purpose

Demonstrates how to structure a Finstein-compliant company profile presentation.

## Required Sources

- `knowledge/brand/`
- `knowledge/visual-identity/`
- `knowledge/design/typography.md`
- `knowledge/design/colors.md`
- `knowledge/presentation-standards/`
- `knowledge/checklists/presentation-checklist.md`

## Slide Outline

1. Cover slide
   - Finstein logo
   - presentation title
   - concise subtitle
2. Organization overview
   - company positioning
   - services summary
3. Mission and vision
   - approved mission
   - approved vision
4. Core capabilities
   - AI
   - analytics
   - cybersecurity
   - digital transformation
5. Values
   - integrity
   - innovation
   - customer success
   - excellence
6. Service areas
   - concise cards using approved spacing and radius
7. Proof points
   - statistics or outcome statements
8. Closing slide
   - contact or next-step message
