# Template Maintenance Process

This document defines the maintenance architecture for future templates.

## Purpose

Ensure templates remain current, reusable, reviewed, and aligned with the Design Kit.

## Maintenance Lifecycle

1. Draft template proposal.
2. Confirm category and owner.
3. Add required metadata.
4. Link dependencies.
5. Review structure.
6. Validate against future checklists.
7. Approve and release.
8. Monitor usage and feedback.
9. Update or deprecate as needed.

## Review Requirements

Future templates should be reviewed for:

- structure
- completeness
- brand alignment
- accessibility
- writing quality
- design token compatibility
- AI usability
- version impact

## Owner Responsibilities

Template owners are responsible for:

- keeping templates current
- coordinating reviews
- documenting changes
- updating dependencies
- requesting deprecation when needed

## Update Frequency

Review active templates at least quarterly or when dependent brand, token, prompt, or checklist guidance changes.

## Milestone Boundary

This maintenance process is architectural. It does not implement automated review workflows.
