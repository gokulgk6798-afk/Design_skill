# Brand Documentation Specification

## Purpose

Define the required structure for future brand documentation, including organization identity, brand principles, visual identity rules, and brand governance.

## Audience

- Brand Owner
- Marketing team
- Design team
- Content team
- AI assistants
- External contributors with approved access

## Scope

Includes the documentation contract for brand guidance. Does not include actual brand values, mission, logo rules, or organization-specific positioning.

## Contents

- brand overview
- mission, vision, and values reference
- brand personality
- positioning and messaging hierarchy
- visual identity references
- logo usage references
- brand governance rules
- approved and restricted usage model

## Dependencies

- `knowledge/` for future brand knowledge
- `assets/` for future approved brand assets
- `docs/documentation/writing-guidelines.md`
- `docs/documentation/colors.md`
- `docs/documentation/typography.md`

## Update Frequency

On brand change.

## Owner

Brand Owner.
