# Writing Guidelines Documentation Specification

## Purpose

Define the required structure for future writing guidelines, including voice, tone, terminology, UX writing, document writing, and AI-generated copy review.

## Audience

- Content Owner
- Brand Owner
- UX Owner
- Product teams
- Marketing
- Sales
- AI assistants

## Scope

Includes the documentation contract for writing standards. Does not include actual voice, tone, terminology, or copy rules.

## Contents

- voice and tone model
- terminology rules
- UX writing rules
- document writing rules
- presentation writing rules
- error and empty state copy guidance
- AI-generated copy review expectations
- examples and anti-pattern references

## Dependencies

- `knowledge/` for future writing and content knowledge
- `docs/documentation/brand.md`
- `docs/documentation/ux.md`
- `docs/documentation/documents.md`
- `docs/documentation/presentations.md`

## Update Frequency

On brand change or content standard change.

## Owner

Content Owner.
