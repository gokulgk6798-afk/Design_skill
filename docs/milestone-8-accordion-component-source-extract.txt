Can I use Flowbite in open-source projects?
Generally, it is accepted to use Flowbite in open-source projects, as long as it is not a UI 
library, a theme, a template, a page-builder that would be considered as an alternative to 
Flowbite itself.
With that being said, feel free to use this design kit for your open-source projects.
Find out more information by reading the release.
