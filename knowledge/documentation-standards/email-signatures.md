## Purpose

Define source-backed email signature rules from the Finstein logo guide.

## Required Content

Use the information from the Finstein business card as a guide when creating a personal email signature.

Include relevant contact information such as:

- telephone number
- fax number, if applicable
- mailing address
- email address

State the job title exactly as it appears on the business card.

## Restrictions

Do not use:

- quotations
- mottoes
- taglines

## Typography

Use Arial only.

Recommended font size:

- 9 pt
- 10 pt

## Color

- Name: bold and black
- Sublines: gray `#A4ACB2`
- Hyperlinks: blue `#03ABEC`

No other colors or combinations are permitted.

## Alignment

Left-justify all lines of information.
