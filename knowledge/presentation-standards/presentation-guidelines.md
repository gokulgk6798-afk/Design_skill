## Purpose

Define initial presentation guidance derived from the approved Finstein presentation source.

## Source Basis

These guidelines are based on the provided firm profile deck. They should be treated as initial source-derived standards, not a complete presentation design system.

## Recommended Slide Types

Future presentation templates should support:

- cover slide
- about-us slide
- mission slide
- growth or timeline slide
- services overview slide
- capability detail slide
- products overview slide
- product detail slide
- why-us slide
- specialist unit slide
- team strength slide
- closing slide

## Content Hierarchy

Slides should generally use:

- a short title
- concise supporting text
- grouped capability lists
- proof points where available
- section-based storytelling

## Narrative Order

For firm-profile and sales-introduction decks, use this broad order:

1. Who we are
2. What we believe or aim to deliver
3. How we have grown
4. What we do
5. Where we have depth
6. What products or platforms we offer
7. Why clients should trust us
8. What makes the team distinctive
9. Closing

## Proof Point Usage

Use only approved proof points from source material.

Current approved proof points from the source include:

- employee scale
- practice leadership
- referral percentage
- project count
- certifications referenced in the source
- multi-disciplinary credentials and expertise

## AI Generation Rules

AI assistants must:

- preserve the source-derived narrative unless instructed otherwise
- ask for missing audience, objective, and source material
- include an approved Finstein logo in generated presentation files
- select the logo using `assets/logos/logo-assets.json`
- use the dark logo on light backgrounds and the white logo on dark or deep-blue backgrounds
- report a blocker if the presentation output format cannot embed the approved PDF logo files
- avoid adding visual rules not supported by source or approved knowledge
- keep metrics and certifications source-bound
- identify slide sections that require updated owner approval
