## Purpose

Capture the presentation structure and content pattern from the approved Finstein firm profile deck.

## Source

Primary source: `assets/source-materials/finstein-presentation.pdf`

## Deck Type

Company profile / firm profile presentation.

## Source-Derived Slide Sequence

The source deck follows this high-level sequence:

1. Cover slide
2. About us
3. Mission
4. Growth story
5. Services overview
6. Application development domains
7. Digital transformation services
8. Cybersecurity services
9. 3D printing services
10. AR and VR services
11. AI and ML services
12. Design services
13. Data visualization value statement
14. Products overview
15. Product and platform offerings
16. Finsoc product slide
17. RequestLY product slide
18. Why us
19. Creative Intelligence Unit
20. Team strength statement
21. Thank-you closing

## Recommended Company Profile Structure

Future company profile decks should preserve this narrative flow unless a task-specific template requires a different order:

- identity and title
- firm credibility and proof points
- mission
- growth or maturity story
- service portfolio
- service-specific capability slides
- product portfolio
- differentiation and why-us rationale
- specialist unit or unique capability highlight
- team strength
- closing slide

## Slide Content Patterns

Use these source-derived content patterns:

- Start with a clear firm-profile cover.
- Use an about-us slide to combine scale, credentials, certifications, and positioning.
- Use a mission slide early in the deck.
- Use timeline or milestone framing for growth.
- Use portfolio slides for services and products.
- Use focused slides for major capabilities.
- Use a why-us slide to connect technology, domain, process, finance, security, and privacy.
- Use a final team-strength or confidence-building slide before closing.

## Messaging Themes

The deck emphasizes:

- innovation-led delivery
- transforming business challenges into opportunities
- client process and performance improvement
- technology, risk, cybersecurity, and financial process expertise
- integrated multi-disciplinary delivery
- security and privacy
- custom applications
- analytics and reporting
- design and creative intelligence

## Presentation Writing Guidance

Use concise slide titles and short supporting statements.

Prefer:

- direct capability labels
- concise proof points
- short explanatory paragraphs where context is needed
- grouped capability lists
- strong section titles for service areas

Avoid:

- unsupported claims
- invented metrics
- unapproved client references
- unverified certifications
- adding service areas not present in approved source material
