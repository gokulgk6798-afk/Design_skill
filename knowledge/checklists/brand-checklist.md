## Objective

Validate that an artifact follows approved Finstein brand standards.

## Checklist

- Uses approved mission, vision, values, culture, and brand personality where brand narrative is required.
- Uses only approved logo assets and formats.
- Applies logo clearspace, minimum size, approved versions, and misuse rules.
- Uses approved brand colors and semantic color roles.
- Uses approved typography and Roboto presentation rules where applicable.
- Uses approved terminology and brand voice.
- Avoids unsupported company claims, certifications, client names, or legal statements.

## Pass Criteria

Pass when all applicable brand rules are followed and unsupported claims are absent.

## Escalation

Escalate to the Brand Owner when logo usage, claims, or brand positioning are unclear.
