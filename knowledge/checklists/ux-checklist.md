## Objective

Validate that user experiences are clear, task-focused, consistent, and easy to understand.

## Checklist

- Uses clear user goals and avoids unnecessary complexity.
- Uses approved terminology consistently.
- Uses sentence case and approved date/time formats.
- Labels are short, descriptive, and scannable.
- Button labels begin with clear actions.
- Error messages explain the issue and recovery path.
- Empty states explain the state and provide a next step.
- Notifications communicate meaningful updates.
- Confirmation dialogs are used only for important or destructive actions.
- User flows avoid unnecessary cognitive effort.

## Pass Criteria

Pass when copy and flow guidance help users understand what happened and what to do next.

## Escalation

Escalate to the UX Owner or Content Owner when terminology, flow, or content intent is unclear.
