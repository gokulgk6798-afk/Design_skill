## Objective

Validate that artifacts follow approved accessibility standards.

## Checklist

- Text maintains sufficient contrast against its background.
- Approved brand color combinations are used.
- Information is not communicated through color alone.
- Semantic colors are paired with icons, labels, or descriptive text.
- Typography is readable at intended viewing size.
- Heading hierarchy is clear.
- Interactive elements are visually distinguishable.
- Disabled, active, and selected states are clear.
- Icons include labels where necessary.
- Charts and diagrams include labels or legends.
- Layouts maintain adequate spacing and hierarchy.

## Pass Criteria

Pass when the artifact is perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust according to the approved standards.

## Escalation

Escalate implementation-level ARIA, keyboard, or screen reader details when they are not defined by the Design Kit.
